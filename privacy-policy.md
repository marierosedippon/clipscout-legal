# Privacy Policy for ClipScout

**Last updated: April 24, 2026**

ClipScout ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by ClipScout. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS app.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
When you create an account, we collect your email address and password via Firebase Authentication.

**Photo and Video Library**
ClipScout requests access to your photo and video library solely to perform AI-powered searches on your device. Photos and video frames are temporarily sent to Google's Gemini API to analyze whether they match your search query. We do not store, retain, or share your photos or videos.

**Usage Data**
Firebase may collect anonymous usage analytics such as app crashes and performance data.

**Purchase Information**
If you subscribe to ClipScout Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple through In-App Purchases. We do not store your payment details.

## How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate your account
- To perform AI-powered searches of your photo library
- To process and manage your subscription
- To improve app performance and fix bugs

## Third Party Services

ClipScout uses the following third party services:

- **Firebase** (Google) — authentication and analytics: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Gemini API** (Google) — AI image analysis: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms
- **Apple In-App Purchases** — subscription billing: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy

## Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

ClipScout is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
