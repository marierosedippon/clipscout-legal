Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Information We Collect

ClipScout may collect information you provide directly, information created through your use of the app, and technical information needed to operate the service.

  • Account information such as email address, display name, profile photo, creator type, social handles, and location if you add them.
  • Camera-roll video metadata and sampled frames only when you start Search, Auto-Scout, upload, save, post, or otherwise choose to use ClipScout features.
  • AI-generated tags, scores, clip rankings, search terms, saves, posts, likes, comments, shares, and app activity.
  • Affiliate, referral, onboarding, analytics, device, and purchase-related information when applicable.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide ClipScout features, find relevant clips, personalize results, operate accounts, process payments, improve search quality, prevent abuse, support users, and understand app performance.

Camera Roll and AI

ClipScout does not scan your camera roll until you grant Photos permission and start a feature that needs scanning. For AI analysis, ClipScout may process sampled frames from videos rather than uploading entire videos by default. Full or additional media processing may require your action or a clear feature flow.

Public and Private Content

Your camera-roll clips are private by default. Other users can see content only when you choose to post or share it publicly. Public posts, profile details, likes, comments, and shares may be visible to other users.

Service Providers

We may use service providers for authentication, hosting, databases, analytics, AI processing, storage, subscriptions, notifications, and crash reporting. These providers process information as needed to operate ClipScout.

Data Retention and Deletion

We keep information as long as needed to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, and improve the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information by contacting us.

Your Choices

You can control Photos access in iOS Settings, edit profile information in the app, delete posts, sign out, and manage subscriptions through Apple.

Children

ClipScout is not intended for children under 13. Do not use ClipScout if you are under 13.
